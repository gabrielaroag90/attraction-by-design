What is Attraction by Design?

Attraction by Design is the concept of design being used to call your attention. The colors chosen, the typography used, the styles laid out; it all should call your attention, in any way possible, if it is well done.

Why Attraction by Design?

The name comes from my belief that design can convey multiple feelings and emotions the creator is feeling or trying to convey.
